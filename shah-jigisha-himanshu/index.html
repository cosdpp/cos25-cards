About Me

I move businesses forward through strategic transformation, whether in a boardroom presentation that shifts company direction, a digital workshop that unlocks new revenue streams, or that one perfectly executed strategy that changes an organization's trajectory. I believe in the power of digital fluency to bridge worlds, connecting financial rigor with innovative technology, Eastern business discipline with Western creative approaches, and complex data with human understanding.
My journey spans over 15 years of translating numbers into narratives and strategies into systems. From developing financial literacy programs at Mahindra to leading digital rebranding for luxury jewelry brands, I have mastered the art of making the complex accessible. My book, Seamless Flow, captures this philosophy: it's about creating purposeful momentum in business and life, where strategy meets execution with intention. Today, I blend data driven insights with human centered design to help organizations navigate digital transformation. Whether consulting for banks like IDBI, training retail teams, or mentoring entrepreneurs at NMIMS, I focus on one thing: creating clarity in complexity.
