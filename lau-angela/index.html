About me

I want to move people – through stories, podcasts, workshops, or that one perfectly chosen sentence.

I believe that the right tone can make the world a better place. With an interdisciplinary background in design, communication, and digital media, and many years of experience in higher education, I explore how content works – and where there is still potential for greater clarity, connection, and relevance. Whether I’m developing a narrative, rethinking the communication of a degree programme, or working on an artistic project, my goal remains the same: to bring focus to what we share with the world.

This is what I want to deepen further – through a master's degree in Content Strategy.
